Welcome to Sri Yogeswara PG

We Take Care of Your Comforts

Welcome to Sri Yogeswara PG, the top-rated PG for Girls/Boys in Bangalore! Our comfortable and fully furnished PG accommodations are designed to meet all your needs, providing a safe and secure living environment.

  • Prime Locations: Our PGs are conveniently located in the Marathahalli and Bellandur areas of Bangalore, offering easy access to public transportation, restaurants, and shopping centers.
  • Room Options: We offer a variety of rooms to choose from, including single and shared accommodations for girls and boys. Each room is equipped with modern amenities to ensure your comfort.
  • Modern Amenities: Enjoy the convenience of amenities such as a fridge, washing machine, clothes dryer, 24/7 hot water, gym facilities, Wi-Fi, RO water, and 24/7 power backup. Inside the rooms, you'll find a smart TV, TV stand, computer table, chair, wardrobe, and a comfortable spring mattress.
  • Safety and Security: Your comfort and safety are our top priorities. We provide round-the-clock security and CCTV surveillance to ensure a secure living environment. Our maintenance staff is always available to address any concerns.
  • Common Facilities: Our PGs offer common areas where you can relax and socialize with fellow residents. You'll have access to a fully equipped kitchen, dining area, table tennis, and foosball for your entertainment.
  • Dedicated Staff: Our dedicated staff is committed to providing excellent service and assistance whenever you need it. We are here to ensure your stay is comfortable and hassle-free.
  • Suitable for All: Whether you're a student, a working professional, or simply seeking a comfortable place to call home, Sri Yogeswara PG is the top-rated choice for girls and boys

Don't wait any longer! Book your stay with us today and experience the ultimate PG living experience in Bangalore. We take pride in being the top-rated PG for Girls/Boys in the city and look forward to welcoming you to Sri Yogeswara PG.
